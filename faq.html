General Questions

What eCommerce platforms do you integrate with?

Currently we only integrate with Shopify.

We will be planning to roll out for other platforms such as WooCommerce and Big Commerce.

Which countries do you deliver to?

Currently we can only deliver to the United States. We will be adding Europe and Australia to our delivering countries very soon.

What are the estimated delivery times?

Orders take approximately 8 to 14 working days to deliver.

We’ll break down each process:

  • 3-5 working days production in our China factory
  • 2-4 working days FedEx® delivery from China to our USA fulfillment center
  • 3-5 working days USPS® First Class Mail shipping

When will my customers receive tracking information?

Once your order has landed in our USA fulfillment center and is sent out by USPS your customers are automatically notified with a USPS tracking number on the email they provided in their order.

What is the minimum number of sales I need to make?

You can sell as little as 1 unit. And you only pay when an order has been submitted to Subliminator.

Is there a monthly fee to use the Sublminator App?

No, our app is completely free to use. You only pay when an order has been submitted to Subliminator.

Can I add my own branding?

Yes, you can upload your own branding in the Subliminator App for each of our products. Your branding gets printed inside the product.

How do I order a sample?

Once you’ve created and published a product in the Subliminator App, you can create a manual order for that product inside Shopify:

  • Go to Shopify > Orders > Create Order
  • Select the product(s) you want to receive and add your address in the ‘Find or create a customer’ section.
  • Mark the order as paid after entering the address. If an order is marked as paid with an incomplete address, our system will ignore the order.
  • Subliminator will then bill you for the base cost + shipping and send you your sample.

Common mistakes

  • Do NOT change the vendor, “subliminator”
  • Do NOT edit the variants/SKU’s
  • Do NOT duplicate a product
  • Do NOT remove product tags

What if an order hasn’t arrived?

IF TRACKING SHOWS DELIVERED

Have your customers check with their local post office and/or neighbours, as often this is where ‘lost’ orders seem to end up.

At peak times (like Christmas and other holidays), please do allow a little longer time for your order to arrive.

IF TRACKING SHOWS RETURNED TO SENDER - INCORRECT ADDRESS

We will not refund nor assume liability for orders that are lost due to the incorrect shipping address provided. Sellers may request a reshipment attempt but will be responsible for fees associated with additional delivery attempts.

IF TRACKING SHOWS NO INFO

If an order has not been successfully delivered and it has been more than 60 days since the order was processed, the seller may request a refund or have the order resent free of charge.

Do you accept returns?

Unfortunately, we are not able to accept returns for any reason. This is because our products are custom made, printed on demand items.

Because of the way Subliminator works, there is an infinite number of designs that could be produced. As such, it would be impossible to sort, store and resell unwanted returns on behalf of all our partners.

Order Shipping

Which countries do you deliver to?

Currently we can only deliver to the United States. We will be adding Europe and Australia to our delivery countries very soon.

What are the estimated delivery times on my orders?

Orders take approximately 8 to 14 days to deliver.

We’ll break down each process:

  • 3-5 days production in our China factory
  • 2-4 days FedEx® delivery from China to our USA fulfillment center
  • 3-5 days USPS® First Class Mail shipping

How much does shipping cost?

There are no shipping fees. Our product base cost is including shipping to the United States. So there are no hidden or additional costs you need to worry about.

To get a more detailed guide on how much each product costs, you can visit our products & pricing page.

Do you offer tracked delivery?

Yes we do. Once your order has arrived in our fulfillment center in the USA, we send it out using USPS First Class Mail that comes with a tracking number. This tracking number is automatically sent to your customer.

How do I track my customers order?

All tracking numbers are automatically sent to your customers. But you may also find the tracking for each of your orders in the Subliminator App in the ‘Orders’ page.

Duties, tax and charges

Custom duties, tax, and charges are not included in our base cost. This is the responsibility of the customer.

Where do your products ship from?

We have our own manufacturing facility in China, from where we ship all orders by FedEx® to our fulfillment center in the United States. And from there they are individually sent by USPS® First Class Mail with tracking.

We accept all major credit cards as well as PayPal for payments of your products.

When will I be charged?

You will be charged every 6 hours when you make a sale on one of our products.

You will be sent a billing statement by email every 24 hours if you have made a sale on one of our products.

What do your products cost?

How do I mark a product as fulfilled in my store?

We mark orders as fulfilled on Shopify automatically for you after an order is shipped. If you have set Shopify to automatically mark orders as fulfilled, we can not forward tracking information to your end customer via Shopify’s built-in shipping notification system.

Order Processing

What happens when a customer places an order?

  • When one of your customers purchases an item that you have published using the Subliminator app, the order is automatically synced with our system.
  • We print, cut and sew your custom print-on-demand product according to your and your customer’s specifications within 3 to 5 days.
  • The item is then packed and shipped by FedEx® to our fulfillment center in the United States which takes approximately 2 to 4 days to arrive.
  • From there it is sent by USPS® First Class Mail to your customer which takes approximately 3 to 5 days to arrive. When it has been dispatched, the order status in your account will automatically update to ‘Fulfilled’.
  • You take the entire payment through Shopify, and we bill you separately for the base cost + shipping.

Will my customers know that you fulfill my orders?

No, your customers will not know that Subliminator fulfills your orders. We ship all our products in blank poly bags with no Subliminator branding. And you even have the option to add your own branding inside the products.

How do I cancel an order?

Contact our support team through support@subliminator.com as soon as you know you want to cancel an order.

You have 24 hours to cancel your order before it’s too late. We will cancel it and refund you, no questions asked within the first 24 hours. After that, all orders will be processed and shipped.

Please note that cancelling an order in your Shopify account will ONLY work BEFORE we mark an order as in production. After we mark as in production, it will NOT override the status in our app, and the order will still process.

We will not be notified of changes you make within your Shopify account and the order will still be shipped (and charged to your account) if you don’t reach out to us.

How do I edit an order?

You have maximum 24 hours to change or cancel an order after it has been placed.

If changes need to be made to the order please email support@subliminator.com and give them the following details:

  1. Name and email on the order
  2. Order number
  3. The changes that need to be made

It is important that you act swiftly, however, because you only have 24 hours to change or cancel an order.

Why is my order status still ‘pending’?

If an order has been processing for more than 2 days there might be a problem. Get in touch with our support team and we’ll look into what the issue could be.

In most cases it could be one of the following:

  • Connectivity issues. If Shopify goes down around the time the order was placed, it may not be transmitted to us correctly.
  • Billing problems. If a card charge fails, your account will be temporarily placed on hold until the payment is successful. Make sure that your credit card and/or PayPal information is up to date in the Subliminator App.

Please NOTE: We are not liable for losses due to delays caused by delinquent payments.

Can I sell offline?

Yes you can! Follow the instructions below to manually create an order.

Once you’ve published a product using the Subliminator App, you can create a manual order for that product inside Shopify:

  1. Come out of the Subliminator App and go to Shopify > Orders > Create Order
  2. Select the product(s) you want to receive and add your address in the ‘Find or create a customer’ section.
  3. Then simply mark the order as paid - but you must only do this after entering the address. If an order is marked as ‘Paid’ with an incomplete address, our system will ignore the order.
  4. We then bill you for the basecost + shipping.

Are there any color restrictions on my design files?

We can print as many colors as you want on your product designs as well as complex images. However, you must make sure you submit your files in .JPG format in at least 150 DPI for accurate and sharp printing results.

What image size should I use?

Please download our design templates from our products & pricing page, or you can download them inside the Subliminator App when you click on one of our products.

Make sure you do not adjust the image size of the design templates, and remove the guidelines when saving to .JPG format.

What DPI should I use?

Designs should use our design templates from our products & pricing, or you can download them inside the Subliminator App when you click on one of our products. The DPI for all our templates is set at 150 DPI.

General Questions

How can I make a product or feature request?

We’d love to hear what features or products you want Subliminator to add! Let us know by sending us a message on our contact page.

Can I sell other products together with my Subliminator products?

Yes. Our products are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing store.

They act just like normal products, so you can edit them, categorize them, and place them in collections with other products.

How do I hide a published product from my store?

Inside your Shopify dashboard go to Shopify > Products and click on the product you wish to hide. On the right hand side of the screen you will see a panel titled ‘Visibility’. Uncheck the ‘Online Store’ and click ‘Save’ in the top right corner.

Or click the calendar icon next to ‘Online Store’ and set a publish date to automatically publish the product at a set time in the future.

Can I change the photos for my products?

Yes you can. Follow the instructions below to do so:

  • Inside your Shopify dashboard go to Shopify > Products
  • Click on the product you’d like to edit/add the photos for.
  • In the images section, you can click and drag the photos around to change the order
  • Hover over an image to be given the option to Preview / Edit / Edit Text / Delete an image.
  • You can also add new images to the existing ones.
  • When you’re finished click ‘Save’ in the top right corner, and you’re all set.

Customer Support

Will my customer receive the tracking number for their order?

Yes, your customers will automatically receive the tracking number by email for their placed order. Please note that tracking is sent to your customer once it arrives in our USA fulfillment center to ensure that the tracking number works and shows the correct info.

You are also able to see all the tracking details for all your orders inside the Subliminator App under the “Orders” tab.

My customers are getting impatient. What should I do?

If your customers are getting impatient while waiting for their order to arrive, don’t worry. Most of the time your customer just wants to know somebody in your company cares and is there to let them know their item(s) are on their way.

We provide customer support scripts you can use to keep your customers calm until their goods arrive. You can download the script HERE.

I need help with a customer’s order. Who should I contact?

You can always reach us from our contact page.

Or via our Live Chat support located at the bottom of every page on our website and inside the Subliminator App.

Please provide customer order number, customer email, and reason for support. We will either reply LIVE or within 6-12 hours.
