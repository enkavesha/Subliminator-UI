Order Processing

Automatically Process Orders

By default the Subliminator system will automatically batch process your orders. You can pause/stop this process by selecting "No" from the drop-down. Processing will only resume when you set the system to "Yes". Orders can only be processed automatically.

Daily Charge Limit

This is the maximum amount in dollars we will charge on your card per day. The automatic processing will not go over this dollar limit in any single day. Leave the box blank for "no limit".

Notifications

When an order is detected, a message will be sent to your e-mail address.

Receipts

A detailed receipt will be sent to your e-mail address when a charge is processed.

Timezone

All orders and export data will be displayed in your local time zone.
